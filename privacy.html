Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ClawMiner (operated by Omnis Network LTD). We respect your privacy and are fully committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension operates and what data it processes.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We believe in absolute sandbox isolation. The ClawMiner Extension DOES NOT collect, access, or store:
- Your browsing history or visited URLs.
- Your cookies, passwords, or keystrokes.
- Any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as your name, email, or IP address (unless explicitly provided for waitlists).

3. Data We Process

To function as an idle compute diagnostic node, the extension only generates and transmits:
- A randomly generated anonymous Node ID (e.g., node-123xyz).
- Basic telemetry pings (uptime status and extension version) to our servers to verify network stability.

4. Use of Idle Bandwidth

By installing ClawMiner, you consent to participate in our decentralized routing network. The extension uses minimal idle bandwidth to perform background network diagnostics. This process operates securely within the Chrome Sandbox restrictions.

5. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at compliance@clawminer.network.
